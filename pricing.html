Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our pricing

Do you offer custom packages?

Yes! We understand that every business has unique needs. Contact us for a custom quote tailored to your specific requirements.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept bank transfers, credit cards, and other digital payment methods. Payment terms can be discussed based on project scope.

Is there a minimum contract period?

For web development, it's a one-time project. For management and SEO services, we recommend a minimum 6-month commitment to see meaningful results.

Are there any hidden fees?

No, all our pricing is transparent. We'll discuss all costs upfront before starting any project. Additional features or scope changes will be quoted separately.

Do you offer refunds?

We stand behind our work. If you're not satisfied, we'll work with you to address any issues. Refund policies are discussed in our service agreement.

Can I upgrade or downgrade my plan?

Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade your management and SEO packages at any time with 30 days' notice.
